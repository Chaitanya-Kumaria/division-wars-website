Rules
Division Wars 2025 – Women’s cricket
Match Format
●	Matches will be 8 overs per side in the First Round.
●	VENUE: BHAVAN’S GROUND
●	Qualification:
●	Top team qualifies directly for the Final.
●	2nd and 3rd ranked teams qualify for the Semi-final.
Power Plays
●	Mandatory Power Play: 2 overs per innings.
●	During Power Play: only 2 fielders allowed outside the 30-yard circle.
●	Non-Power Play overs: minimum 4 fielders must remain inside the 30-yard circle.
Bowling Restrictions
●	Maximum of 2 overs per bowler for any 2 bowlers.
●	Remaining bowlers can bowl a maximum of 1 over each.
●	One bouncer allowed per over.
●	Chucking strictly not allowed — action will be penalized immediately. Any chucking incident will be treated as a No ball
Batting & Fielding
●	Runners for batsmen are not allowed.
●	Gloves are not allowed for both batsmen and keeper .
●	Nail spikes are not allowed — only sports shoes permitted.
●	Byes and leg byes  are allowed as per ICC rules.
●	Only wooden bats are allowed
Ball & Equipment
●	Matches will be played with a Tenex tennis ball.
Tie-Breakers
●	In case of a tie:
1.	Super Over will decide the winner.
2.	If Super Over is tied → another Super Over.
3.	If still tied → Bowl Out will decide the winner.
Time Regulations
●	Each team must complete their 8 overs in 40 minutes (approx. 5 minutes per over).
Umpiring
●	On-field umpires’ decision is final and binding.
●	Umpiring to be done by PGP-2s
●	Neutral umpires will be appointed by the organizing committee.
General Rules
●	All ICC rules apply, except LBW is not applicable.
●	Mankading is allowed
●	Innings break: 5–10 minutes.
●	Scoring: All matches will be scored on the CricHeroes app.
●	Only single end playing would be allowed
Group Stage Points
●	Each team plays 2 matches in the group stage.
●	Points system:
○	Win = 2 points
○	Loss = 0 points
●	If teams are tied on points, Net Run Rate (NRR) will decide ranking
