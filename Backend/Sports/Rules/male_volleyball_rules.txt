Rules (Male):
1. Team size: There will be 1 team per division, comprising of 8 minimum – 10 maximum number of individuals
2. Substitution of players is allowed in between the matches after every point. Infinite substitution is allowed.
3. Each match consists of 3 sets of 15 points each.
5. All 6 players are supposed to serve.
6. Back-row attacks from beyond the front line are allowed.
7. Any net touch is a foul.
8. A toss will be conducted to decide who serves first.
9. In the case of delays caused because of players not arriving on time the opposing team will gain 1 point per minute of delay in the game
10. All other standard rules will apply.
11. The referees’ decision will be deemed final in case of any discrepancy, and that will stand above the challenge of any team.
12. 2 time outs allowed per set, each time out is for 60 seconds only.
