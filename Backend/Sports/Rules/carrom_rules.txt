# Carrom Rules

1. **Format**: Singles or Doubles matches.
2. **Scoring**: Best of 3 games, 25 points per game.
3. **Setup**: Standard carrom board with 9 white, 9 black, and 1 red coin.
4. **Fouls**: Pocketing striker, pocketing opponent's coin, etc.
5. **Winning**: First player/team to reach 25 points wins the game.
