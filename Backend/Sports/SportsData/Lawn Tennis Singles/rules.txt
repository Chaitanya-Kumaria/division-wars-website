Lawn Tennis Singles Rules

1. Format
   - Singles category.
   - Matches played as best of 3 sets (or specific tournament rules).
   - Standard tennis scoring (15, 30, 40, Deuce, Advantage).

2. Points
   - Win: 2 points
   - Loss: 0 points

3. General
   - All standard ITF rules apply.
   - Umpire's decision is final.
